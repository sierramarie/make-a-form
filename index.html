Dreamcatchers!

According to Native American legend, dream catchers were made and used to filter out bad dreams for young children. They were made with willow hoops, cords made from plants, feathers and charms/beads symbolizing different meanings.


"Only good dreams would be allowed through. The bad dreams would get stuck in the net and disappear with the light of the day. The good dreams would slide down the feathers to the sleeper."
